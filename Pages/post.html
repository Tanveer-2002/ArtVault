The Scream
Edvard MunchArtist

Details:

The Scream is an iconic Expressionist artwork created by Norwegian painter Edvard Munch between 1893 and 1910. The image of a figure clutching its face and crying out against a swirling, blood-red sky has become a universal symbol of modern human anxiety, alienation, and existential dread.
